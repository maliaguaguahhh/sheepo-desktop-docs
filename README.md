### 1.1 Data Types We Collect
We use **Google Firebase Analytics** to collect the following categories of data:

* **Usage Data:**
    * **Session Metrics:** Duration of focus and break sessions, completion rates, and "skip" actions.

* **Identifiers:**
    * We use a random, pseudonymous **App Instance ID** generated by Firebase. This identifier allows us to count unique users and analyze retention but is **not** linked to your hardware serial numbers (UDID) or your real-world identity.
* **Diagnostics:**
    * Crash reports and performance data to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

* **To Ensure Stability:** Crash reports help us quickly identify and fix technical issues.

We do **not** use this data for third-party advertising, profiling, or tracking you across other apps and websites.
